2.Climate Policy Council

While each country has its set legal frameworks to govern climate change, less is known on how they conduct their duties or how they make progress on their climate advocacy.

In this project, we advocate for the creation of a climate policy council to hold the government accountable and analyze policy set by the government and how it aligns to climate goals set by the Parliament and government. The Council will hold the government accountable through:

  • Bringing attention to societal effects of proposed policy
  • Recognizing deficient policy
  • Analyzing short- and long-term targets with climate and budget policy goals in mind
  • Promoting discussion on climate policy in society

The climate policy council need not be within a country in limitation of financial resources. We have countries such as Sweden, Denmark, and Chile which are leading in climate policy and they could come in agreement with developing countries and hold them accountable in their going green.

Their involvement will help in:

  • Assessing the impact of current policies in various sectors on climate objectives
  • Analyzing the societal repercussions of both established and forthcoming policies
  • Recognizing the policy domains requiring supplementary actions to align with climate targets
